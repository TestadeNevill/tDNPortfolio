About Me

Time is against me so I can’t get into too much detail but synchronicity allows me just enough to paint a picture. The hardest part to any goal is proving to yourself first you can achieve it and I have a goal of building a city of the future. My interest in cities has led me through a career specific to how they work and ways to improve them physically and socio-economically. The restoration of our natural environments and an increase in sustainable designs are ideologies I hope to work towards.


I look forward to spending my time working on plans and projects that improve our collective quality of life. In the industry of infrastructure, technology or both, the possibilities of creative solutions are endless and I’m excited to be a part of those who change the world for the better.
